About FirstStep USA

We're dedicated to making your transition to life in the United States as smooth and successful as possible.

Our Mission

Moving to a new country for education is exciting but can also be overwhelming. At FirstStep USA, we provide comprehensive support to international students in all aspects of their American experience.

Housing Assistance

We help you find safe, affordable housing near your campus with trusted landlords.

Financial Guidance

Learn to budget effectively and discover student discounts to make your money go further.

Career Support

Gain access to internship opportunities and job placement assistance tailored to your field.
